About the Placement Management System

Our system streamlines the placement process by connecting students, companies, and administrators through a user-friendly platform. Whether you're a student looking for job opportunities, a company looking for talent, or an admin managing the process, this system simplifies it all.

Features

  • ✔ Easy student and company registration
  • ✔ Job postings and application tracking
  • ✔ Admin dashboard for efficient management
  • ✔ Secure login for students and companies
